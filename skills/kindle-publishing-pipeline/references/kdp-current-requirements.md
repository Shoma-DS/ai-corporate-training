## eBook Manuscript

KDP's eBook guide emphasizes:

- Use proper paragraph indentation and line spacing; do not rely on tab spacing for paragraph indents.
- Apply heading styles to chapter titles so the table of contents can be generated.
- Include front matter such as title page, optional copyright/dedication, and back matter such as author bio or bibliography when appropriate.
- Add page breaks at chapter boundaries.
- Footnotes must work as links.
- Preview before upload and fix errors found by KDP review or preview tools.

Practical production rule: keep the working manuscript in Markdown or DOCX, but prepare a clean reflowable upload file. Avoid layout tricks that break on Kindle devices.

## eBook Cover

KDP's cover image criteria currently include:

- Minimum: 1000 px height and 625 px width.
- Maximum: 10000 px height and 10000 px width.
- Ideal height/width ratio: at least 1.6:1.
- File size: under 50 MB.
- Color profile: RGB.
- KDP notes that minimal compression is preferable and that images should have at least 300 PPI for best results.
- Very light covers can need a narrow medium-gray border so they remain visible on white backgrounds.

Practical production rule: use 2560 x 1600 px or larger at the same 1.6 ratio when possible, keep text legible at thumbnail size, and validate dimensions before upload.

## Paperback

KDP paperback formatting depends on trim size, page count, bleed, and margins. Current KDP guidance says bleed extends printed objects beyond trim so page-edge images/backgrounds reach the edge after cutting.

Current paperback bleed/margin notes from KDP:

- Bleed trim removes 0.125 in / 3.2 mm from top, bottom, and outside edges.
- For bleed setup, add 0.125 in to width and 0.25 in to height.
- Use mirror margins.
- Inside/gutter margin changes with page count.
- Outside margins differ for no-bleed versus bleed.

Practical production rule: do not reuse ebook formatting as print formatting. Create a separate print manuscript/PDF after page count and trim size are known.

## Content, Rights, and AI

Before upload, verify the latest KDP Content Guidelines and account prompts for:

- ownership and publishing rights,
- copyright and trademark risks,
- public domain rules,
- duplicate or low-quality content rules,
- misleading metadata,
- AI-generated or AI-assisted content disclosure,
- adult content, prohibited content, and region-specific restrictions.

Public repository rule for this repo: do not store private client data, unpublished third-party materials, account screenshots containing personal data, royalty reports, tax information, KDP login details, or company-specific sales data in public files.

## Manual Upload Fields to Prepare

Prepare these before opening KDP:

- book title and subtitle,
- series/edition if applicable,
- author and contributors,
- description,
- keywords,
- categories,
- language and reading direction,
- age/audience settings where applicable,
- publishing rights confirmation,
- AI disclosure notes based on current KDP wording,
- manuscript file,
- cover file,
- ISBN/imprint decisions for print,
- territories,
- pricing and royalty choices,
- KDP Select decision.
